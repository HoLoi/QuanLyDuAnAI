10. Gợi ý viết lại Abstract

Có thể viết lại như sau:

Abstract. Project delays remain a persistent challenge in software and organizational project management, leading to cost overruns, resource inefficiency, and reduced delivery quality. While existing AI-based studies in project management mainly focus on delay prediction, effort estimation, and risk forecasting, less attention has been given to explainable delay cause analysis that can support managerial decision-making. This paper proposes a Human-in-the-Loop decision-support framework for delay cause analysis in an AI-integrated project management system. The framework aggregates operational data from project, task, progress, resource, cost, and workflow modules into project-level feature vectors and uses manager-confirmed delay causes as validated labels. A Decision Tree classifier is employed to provide interpretable multi-class delay cause analysis across ten predefined cause categories. In an internal dataset consisting of 460 labeled project samples, the model achieved an accuracy of 86.96% and a Macro F1-score of 87.03%. The results suggest that interpretable tree-based models can provide useful diagnostic support for project managers while preserving transparency and human control. However, the current evaluation is limited by the internal dataset size and requires further validation using larger and more diverse project data. The study contributes a decision-support architecture, a manager-validated labeling pipeline, and an explainable AI approach for delay cause analysis in project management.

Bản này tốt hơn vì có đủ: context, gap, method, dataset, result, contribution, limitation.
